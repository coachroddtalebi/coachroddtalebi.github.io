Coach Rodd Talebi

Varsity Women's Head Coach

coach.roddtalebi@gmail.com

Coach Rodd finished his third (transfer student) and final year as a rower for UCLA in 2014. In that time he medaled at WIRA three times (twice in the heavyweight varsity 8), selected to All WIRA First Team in 2014, competed twice at ACRA with the men's heavyweight varsity 8, and elected as Head Captain for his final season. In 2014, he completed his dual degree in Applied Mathematics and Astrophysics

After graduating, he spent a year as the assistant novice coach ('14-'15 season) at his alma mater, and acted as the first Data Fellow for the City of Los Angeles working directly with the city's Chief Data Officer.

In 2015 he moved to Atlanta to pursue a Masters in Analytics from Georgia Tech. Prior to his arrival, he was asked to join the Varsity Men's squad as an assistant to Coach Michael Gibel. As the first fall season came to a close, Coach Rodd was transferred to the Varsity Women's squad as an assistant to, then Head Coach, Jay Skuban. In the 2017 offseason, after completing his degree and landing a research faculty position with Georgia Tech, Coach Rodd was asked to continue as Head Coach for the Varsity Women's squad.
